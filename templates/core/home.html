About

Where light
becomes language

Amir Parsa is a Tehran-based light designer and curator working at the intersection of visual art, spatial design, and live performance.

His practice centers on creating immersive environments where light is not decoration but material — shaping how people move through, feel, and remember a space.

As a curator, Amir brings together multidisciplinary artists — visual designers, sound artists, and DJs — to build experiences that blur the line between art and event.
